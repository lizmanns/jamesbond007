THE STORY OF THE BOND MUSEUM

The 007 Museum was founded in 1996 by Guntar Walker. He has taken James Bond souvenirs and relics from all over the world and put them up for display in his galleries. He collects everthing from cars and aircrafts from the movie, to bullet-shaped shot glasses and martini glasses for crafting the perfect Vesper Martini. He even hosts parties that serve as an reanactment of the films. During these parties, guests can gamble with actual poker chips and witness James Bond himself (Gunter dressed as him)spying on his enemies. And, of course, merchandise is offered in an array of t-shirts, glassware, and posters. It's the perfect setting to feel like you are a spy in one of the films for an evening.
